Our Range of High-Performance Blades

L-Type Blades

Crafted from high-quality 30MnCrB5 material, L-Type blades are engineered for precision and durability. Designed for deep cuts, these blades ensure only the cutting edge interacts with the soil, minimizing ground compaction while maintaining a self-sharpening profile. Ideal for shallow cultivations of 2 inches or less, they are also perfect for leveling the tillage zone, delivering exceptional performance across various soil conditions.

Super Series Blades

Engineered for toughness and exceptional performance, Super Series blades are designed to penetrate hard or clay-rich soils with ease. Constructed from a specially engineered alloy, the blades feature a thermally sprayed coating applied in a molten state to achieve superior wear resistance against abrasive forces. Additionally, these blades are precision-crafted with a hydraulically induced curve, creating a hybrid profile between L-Type and C-Type designs. This unique geometry enhances soil engagement efficiency, reduces wear rates, and ensures optimal durability for demanding applications.
